About the Creator

I designed this project with the intent to combine my love for tarot and the importance of self-care.

Tarot can be a mirror, not a map. The goal here is self-connection, affirmation, softness, and honesty.

I believe in self love, the power of the universe, and the power of technology. If this project helped you slow down, breathe, or understand yourself even a little more, then it already served its purpose.

You are always loved and welcome here.
