About the Creator

I created this project to blend tarot’s reflective magic with gentle, practical self-care.

Tarot is a mirror, not a map. It reveals possibilities, patterns, and emotions—never demands.

My intention is to offer grounding guidance, soft support, and a place where you can meet yourself with honesty and compassion.

You are always welcome here.
